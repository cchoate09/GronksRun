Privacy Policy for Gronk's Run

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Gronk's Run ("the App") is a mobile game developed by an independent developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores game progress data locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Game progress (level, score, gems, achievements)
  • Game settings (sound preferences, screen shake toggle)
  • Unlocked characters and cosmetic skins
  • Daily login streak and mission progress

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded video advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)

We request non-personalized ads only. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not have user accounts or login systems
  • We do not collect or transmit gameplay data to any server

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App contains no user-generated content, social features, or personal data collection. Rewarded ads are optional and clearly labeled.

Data Retention

All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by clearing the App's data through your device settings or by uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying rewarded video advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
