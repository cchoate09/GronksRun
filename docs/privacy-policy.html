Privacy Policy for Gronk's Run

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Gronk's Run ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information is requested or gathered.

Locally Stored Data

The App stores game progress data (such as level completion, scores, and unlocked characters) locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device settings

Third-Party Advertising

The App displays optional rewarded advertisements provided by Google AdMob. When you choose to watch an ad, Google AdMob may collect certain information in accordance with Google's privacy policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)

We do not control or have access to any data collected by AdMob. Ads are optional and only shown when you actively choose to watch them for in-game rewards. For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and contains only cartoon/fantasy content. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Analytics

The App does not use any analytics services that collect or transmit user data to external servers.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our end. All game data remains on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
